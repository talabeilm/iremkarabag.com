## Overview

A collaborative literary work published in *The Capilano Review*, combining essay and poetry across English, Dene, and Turkish.

## Themes

- Displacement and belonging across linguistic and cultural boundaries
- Colonialism within academic institutions and the struggle for indigenous language reclamation
- The power of multilingual creative expression as a form of resistance and self-reclamation

## Collaboration

This piece was co-authored with Laura Grier, bringing together indigenous and diasporic perspectives on language, displacement, and the possibilities of cross-cultural creative dialogue.
